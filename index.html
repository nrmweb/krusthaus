Our Story

A Bakery Built on Craft & Community

Krust Haus was born out of a love for genuine food — the kind that takes time, care, and real ingredients. Every morning we bake fresh in our Portland Hills kitchen, from buttery croissants to seasonal pastries and artisan bread. No shortcuts, ever.

Baked Fresh Daily
Our ovens run from dawn so you always get it at its best.
Quality Ingredients
Real eggs, fresh produce, and honest sourcing — always.
Great Coffee
Specialty espresso drinks crafted to pair with every bite.
Cozy & Welcoming
A neighbourhood spot where everyone feels at home.
